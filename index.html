Care Process

Sanela Care personalizes insights-based care plan for each member to promote a healthy lifestyle and manage chronic conditions. Our care is designed to help members take control of the treatment, prevent future declines in their health and encourages involvement of the member and family in ongoing self management.

Along with our own network of Primacy Care physicians and specialists, we partner with reputed health care providers to ensure the member gets right care by the right provider, when and where they need it.

Contact us and we will discuss your needs to craft a personalized care plan

Our care team will contact you for an assessment either online, or by phone, or a personal visit with recommended social distancing guidelines. Our team can create a care plan tailored to member’s evolving needs.

Dedicated Primary Care Physician

We match a dedicated primary care physician for your on going care needs who is the best fit for your needs and personality.

We assign a virtual care team that’s accessible 24/7

When you hire us, you receive a full-time care team to assist you remotely. Care Coordinator develops a specialized care plan and provides quality assurance throughout the length of service.

We create care portal account to access up-to-date information

We have designed a proprietary Care Platform, a health portal for all our members, to see health insights, medical reports, prescriptions, reminders, incidents and reports.
